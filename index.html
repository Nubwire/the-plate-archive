About The Plate Archive

Welcome to The Plate Archive Co., a curated collection of antique and vintage ceramic plates, teacups, and collectibles from renowned makers around the world.

Our Story

The Plate Archive Co. is a Federation Collectibles Company dedicated to preserving and sharing the beauty of historical ceramics. Each piece in our collection has been carefully selected for its craftsmanship, provenance, and condition.

What We Offer

  • Rare and collectible patterns from Royal Doulton, Wedgwood, Noritake, and more
  • Authentic antique pieces dating from the 1890s to mid-century
  • Detailed condition reports and honest disclosures
  • Worldwide shipping with careful packaging
  • Expert knowledge and personalized service

Our Commitment

We believe in transparency and authenticity. Every item is photographed in detail, including maker's marks and any condition issues. We provide comprehensive descriptions and are always available to answer questions about our pieces.

Shipping Policy

Domestic Shipping (Australia)

  • Standard Shipping: AUD $15.00 (5-7 business days)
  • Express Shipping: AUD $25.00 (2-3 business days)
  • Free shipping on orders over AUD $150

International Shipping

  • Worldwide shipping available
  • Rates calculated at checkout based on weight and destination
  • Typically 10-21 business days
  • Tracking provided for all international orders

Packaging

Every item is carefully packaged using acid-free tissue paper, bubble wrap, and double-boxed for maximum protection. We have an excellent track record with safe delivery of fragile ceramics.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Customs & Duties

International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees charged by their country. These charges are not included in shipping costs.

Returns & Refunds

Our Guarantee

We stand behind every piece we sell. If an item arrives damaged or is not as described, we will provide a full refund including shipping costs.

Return Policy

  • 14-day return window from date of delivery
  • Items must be in original condition and packaging
  • Buyer pays return shipping unless item is defective or misrepresented
  • Refunds processed within 5 business days of receiving return

Damaged in Transit

If your item arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photos of the damage and packaging. We will arrange a replacement or full refund immediately.

How to Return

  1. Contact us at returns@theplatearchive.co
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We'll provide a Return Authorization number
  4. Package item securely and ship to the address provided
  5. Once received, we'll process your refund
